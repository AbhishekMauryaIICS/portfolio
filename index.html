about

One engineer, four lenses on the same problem.

I started as a developer and kept finding the same pattern: a great product dies quietly without traffic, and a great campaign collapses without a secure, well-built product behind it. So I learned all three sides — building, growing, and defending — instead of outsourcing two of them. Cyber security ties it together: it's the discipline of asking "how could this break" before someone else does, and I bring that question into every line of code and every campaign.

Based in India, working with teams and clients globally — remote-first, deadline-respecting, documentation-writing.
